Top 5

Katibawasan Falls

Katibawasan FallsKatibawasan Waterfall is located in Camiguin Mambajao, the waterfall is 75 meters long, the water slopes down, and the clear water flows straight down from the top.

Because there is no direct sunlight, it is said that the water is relatively cool, so you can play in the water.

There are various flowers and plants, ferns and pebbles growing around the pool, which can be used for a picnic. See more in Katibawasan Falls



Top 4

Tinago Falls

Tinago FallsOut of the city's waterfalls, Tinago Falls is second to Maria Christina Falls as the most-visited tourist spot in that area.

Tourists love the scenic waterfalls coming from the 240feet-high place running down into the deep tranquil water, alluring you to take an instant deep on the water.

Relieve your stress. See more in Tinago Falls



Top 3

MantigueI sland

Mantigue IslandMantigue Island is a small island located in Camiguin Province, Philippines.

Also known as Magsaysay Island, it covers an area of about 4 hectares and is shady with trees and surrounded by white sandy beaches of pink coral sand.

There is a fishing village on the north side of the island.

One side of the island is white sandy beach with coral nearby, while the other side is deep for snorkeling and diving. See more in MantigueI sland



Top 2

The Sunken Cemetery

Sunken CemeteryThe Sunken Cemetery in Camiguin is one of the most popular attractions on the island and an adventure not to be missed.

You can go snorkeling at the Camiguin Sunken Cemetery and spot old tombstones and crosses as well as an abundance of marine life.

Top 1

White Sandy Beach

White Sandy BeachThe white sandy beach in Miganyin is relatively small and can be reached by boat from Miganyin.

The sand of the white beach is very fine and soft.

If you take a picture here, the background is the volcano of Miganyin, which is very spectacular.

Because it is relatively small, it is estimated that the entire beach can be walked around in ten minutes.

There is also an island not far from the white sand beach, which is full of egg-laying birds.
