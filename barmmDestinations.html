Top 5

Tawi-Tawi

Tawi-Tawi-The province lies at the southwestern tip of the country, situated between the Sulu Sea in the north and the Celebes Sea in the south.

It is home to fascinating coral reefs and diverse sea beings. See more in Tawi-Tawi



Top 4

Sulu

Sulu-Sulu is a province of the Philippines in the Sulu Archipelago.

Fishing is the most important industry since the Sulu Sea is one of the richest fishing grounds in the country.

The province also has an extensive pearl industry. See more in Sulu



Top 3

Maguindanao

Maguindanao-Maguindanao is a province in the Philippines situated in the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

The provincial capital is Buluan. See more in Maguindanao



Top 2

Cotabato City

Cotabato City-Cotabato City, officially the City of Cotabato is an independent component city in the Philippine autonomous region of Bangsamoro.

Cotabato City is home to the largest mosque in the Philippines, the Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah Masjid.
See more in Cotabato City



Top 1

Basilan

Basilan-Basilan province is an island province and the largest of the major islands of the Sulu Archipelago.

It features many natural attractions including the lake in the fishing villages, Beaches, and the waterfalls.
See more in Basilan
